Turbulent tides at sea  
Give me some respite   
From me  

O cloud stirring the ocean   
Let me drown in   
Your cauldron deep  

Verdant hills   
drink deep of my potion   
Let me seep into your trees  

Little Bird eats my seeds  
Take me with you    
I have new places to see
